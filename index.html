Path for Every Player

From the first time they touch a pawn to mastering complex tournament strategy, our programs are designed to guide your child every step of the way.

Ages 5-8

Beginner

This is where the magic begins! Our beginner program is built around fun, storytelling, and interactive games to make learning the rules of chess an exciting adventure.

What They'll Learn:

  • The name and movement of every chess piece.
  • Special moves like Castling and En Passant.
  • The basic rules: what "Check" and "Checkmate" mean.
  • Simple strategies and the value of each piece.
  • How to play a full game, start to finish!
Ages 9-12

Intermediate

Once students have mastered the basics, we introduce them to the "why" behind the moves. This class focuses on strategy, tactical patterns, and critical thinking.

What They'll Learn:

  • Key opening principles (control the center, develop pieces).
  • Recognizing common tactics: Forks, Pins, and Skewers.
  • Basic endgame techniques and checkmating patterns.
  • How to analyze a position and form a simple plan.
  • Sportsmanship and learning from losses.
Teens & Tournament Players

Advanced

For dedicated students who are serious about their game. This program is for those playing in tournaments or aiming to achieve a rating. We dive deep into complex theory and high-level strategy.

What They'll Learn:

  • Advanced opening theory and building a repertoire.
  • Deep positional understanding (pawn structures, piece activity).
  • Complex endgame scenarios and calculation.
  • Game analysis and self-evaluation.
  • Tournament preparation and chess psychology.
